THE NATURE OF HUMANS

A human could be walking through a supermarket aisle, they could be creeping through a forest trail. Either setting they'd still be in survival mode. That aisle is built for the foraging of survival aiding tools, all put together by the modern-day hunter-gatherers of the nation to make the hunt easier and to make the hunt secure.

Remember that every time you observe the seemingly perpetual hustle and bustle of humans at your local supermarket, you are witnessing hundreds of thousands of years of primal, ancestral needs that are looking to be met within the elevated human habitat.

Your interaction with the world should be as authentic and clear as this. It isn't enough to passively carry yourself throughout life and forget how things are on a fundamental level. The core of your being should exude beyond a generation of human existence as that way you get the full picture.
