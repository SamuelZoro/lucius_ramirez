INTRODUCTION

The whole point of this discourse is for you to reconsider the nature of human reality. To realise the part you can play, to finding your purpose. The way the rules have been set has generationally rendered us amnesiac to how things are in their most basic, material, unbiased form. They've taken our identity, instead of giving us a sense of who we really are. One of the most honourable, commendable things you can do is to not feel a need to conform to these rules.

The best way to find yourself is to "lose" yourself. To think outside the box. Let go of what is supposedly true about the world and what it means to be human. Hold on to what is deemed a falsehood in our customs. This intimate detachment from the set-in-stone ways allows you to start again from scratch. You're encouraging a novel interaction with the world. You, my friend, are the founder of yourself.
